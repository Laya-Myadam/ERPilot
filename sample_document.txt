1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
================================================================

Denovo Consulting ("Denovo") will implement Oracle Fusion Cloud HCM for Acme
Manufacturing Inc. ("Client") across its three US legal entities located in
Ohio, Texas, and California. The project will replace the Client's current
HR and payroll platform, ADP Workforce Now.

Project Start Date:   April 21, 2025
Target Go-Live Date:  October 1, 2025
Total Project Budget: $2,100,000 (fixed fee)
Contract Type:        Fixed Price

================================================================
2. SCOPE OF SERVICES
================================================================

2.1 MODULES IN SCOPE
- Core HCM (Worker, Employment, Organization Management)
- Oracle Payroll (US)
- Absence Management
- Time and Labor (OTL)

2.2 OUT OF SCOPE
The following are explicitly excluded from this engagement:
- Canada operations (to be addressed in Phase 2)
- Oracle Recruiting Cloud (IRC)
- Oracle Learning Management
- Performance Management
- Compensation Workbench
- Integration with any third-party benefits carrier (benefits carrier
  EDI files are a separate engagement)

2.3 LEGAL ENTITIES IN SCOPE
1. Acme Manufacturing Ohio LLC (Primary entity, 1,400 employees)
2. Acme Texas Operations Inc. (600 employees)
3. Acme California Logistics Inc. (400 employees)
Total in-scope employee count: 2,400 employees

4. PROJECT PHASES AND MILESTONES
================================================================

Phase 1 — Initiation & Discovery (Apr 21 – May 9, 2025)
  - Project kickoff and governance setup
  - Current-state discovery workshops (5 days on-site)
  - Configuration workbook distribution
  Milestone: Discovery complete and workbooks returned — May 9

Phase 2 — Design & Configuration (May 12 – Jul 11, 2025)
  - Core HCM configuration (org structure, jobs, positions)
  - Payroll element setup (earnings, deductions, taxes)
  - Absence plan configuration (6 leave types)
  - OTL work schedules (3-shift manufacturing pattern)
  - Fast Formula development (OT, shift differential)
  Milestone: Configuration complete and workbook signed off — Jul 11

Phase 3 — Data Migration (Jun 2 – Aug 1, 2025)
  - HDL template preparation
  - Data extract from ADP Workforce Now
  - Data cleansing and transformation
  - Mock migration #1 — Jun 30
  - Mock migration #2 — Jul 21
  - Final migration — Aug 1
  Milestone: Data migration sign-off — Aug 1

Phase 4 — Testing (Jul 14 – Aug 29, 2025)
  - System Integration Testing (SIT) — Jul 14–25
  - Parallel Run #1 — Jul 28–Aug 8
  - Parallel Run #2 — Aug 11–22
  - UAT — Aug 25–29
  Milestone: UAT sign-off — Aug 29

Phase 5 — Cutover & Go-Live (Sep 1 – Oct 1, 2025)
  - Cutover planning finalization
  - Final data load — Sep 26–28
  - Go/No-Go review — Sep 29
  - Production go-live — Oct 1
  Milestone: Go-live confirmed — Oct 1

Phase 6 — Hypercare (Oct 1 – Oct 31, 2025)
  - 30 days post-go-live support
  - Daily check-ins for first 2 weeks
  - Issue triage and resolution

================================================================
6. ASSUMPTIONS
================================================================

This SOW is based on the following assumptions. If these prove incorrect,
Denovo reserves the right to issue a Change Request:

1. Client will assign named SMEs who are available a minimum of 50% of
   their time during configuration phases.
2. ADP data extracts will be provided to Denovo in Excel or CSV format
   by May 15, 2025.
3. Client's IT team will provision Oracle Cloud tenant environments
   (Production, Test, Development) by April 28, 2025.
4. The go-live date of October 1, 2025 is fixed and cannot move.
5. Union employees in Ohio (approximately 340 Teamsters Local 436 members)
   will follow standard Oracle payroll configuration. CBA-specific rules
   will be handled via fast formulas, which are in scope.
6. California double-time rules will require legal sign-off from Client's
   HR Legal team. Denovo will build the fast formula; legal approval is
   Client's responsibility.
7. No more than 3 custom integrations are required (401k, benefits carrier
   file, and GL journal entry). Each additional integration is out of scope.

10. RISKS AND MITIGATION
================================================================

Risk 1 — UAT Resource Availability
  Probability: High | Impact: High
  Description: Client has not yet named 2 UAT testers (due May 1).
  Mitigation: Escalate to Jennifer Walsh if names not received by May 1.
  If UAT team is insufficient, go-live may shift by up to 3 weeks.

Risk 2 — ADP Data Quality
  Probability: Medium | Impact: High
  Description: ADP data may have duplicates or missing fields.
  Mitigation: Mock migration #1 on Jun 30 will identify data issues.
  Client must complete data cleansing before Mock #2 (Jul 21).

Risk 3 — California OT Fast Formula Complexity
  Probability: Medium | Impact: Medium
  Description: California double-time calculation requires legal review.
  Mitigation: Begin fast formula development by Jun 2. Legal sign-off
  must be obtained by Jul 1 to avoid SIT delays.

Risk 4 — Oracle Tenant Provisioning Delay
  Probability: Low | Impact: High
  Description: If Oracle takes more than 2 weeks to provision the tenant,
  the Phase 2 start date will slip.
  Mitigation: Client to submit Oracle provisioning request by Apr 23.
